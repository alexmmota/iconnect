IConnect is an application that allows the user to publish on Facebook, Twitter and send emails even when there is not connection with the internet.

On the first access, it's necessary that the user be connected to be able to access his Facebook account or Twitter and allow Iconnect to publish on your profile.

For each post or email is charged the cost of a sms, according to the user's operator telephone plan.
